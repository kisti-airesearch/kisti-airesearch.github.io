Research

From training foundation models on national supercomputers to applying them to real scientific problems — our research covers the full stack of large-scale AI.

🧠

Science-Specialized Large Language Models — KONI

We develop KONI (KISTI Open Natural Intelligence), a family of large language models specialized for the science and technology domain that excel in both Korean and English. KONI models are built through continual pretraining on large scientific corpora, supervised fine-tuning (SFT), and preference optimization (DPO), and are released openly so that anyone can build upon them.

  • Continual pretraining on Korean/English scientific text
  • Instruction tuning and alignment for research-assistant use cases
  • Open release of models on Hugging Face
🔬

AI for Science

We apply large-scale AI to accelerate scientific discovery. Leveraging KISTI's national science and technology information resources, we build LLM-based technologies that help researchers navigate, understand, and generate scientific knowledge.

  • Scientific literature understanding and knowledge extraction
  • Retrieval-augmented question answering over S&T information
  • AI research assistants for the Korean research community
📚

Scientific Data, Corpora & Benchmarks

High-quality data is the foundation of capable models. We construct and openly share large-scale Korean scientific text corpora, instruction datasets, and evaluation benchmarks tailored to the science and technology domain.

  • Large-scale Korean scientific corpora for pretraining
  • Domain instruction datasets for fine-tuning
  • Benchmarks for evaluating scientific knowledge and reasoning
⚡

Large-scale AI Infrastructure & Platforms

Training and serving large models requires serious systems work. Building on KISTI's role as the operator of Korea's national supercomputing infrastructure, we research efficient distributed training, model compression and optimization, and platform technologies for delivering AI services at national scale.

  • Distributed training of foundation models on supercomputers
  • Efficient inference and model optimization
  • AI service platforms for the research ecosystem
